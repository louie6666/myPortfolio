About

I am a 3rd-year IT student. If I’m being honest, I originally chose IT just to get a diploma. But along the way, I realized how cool it actually is. What started as just a goal to graduate turned into a real hobby, and now I’m genuinely interested in how everything works behind the scenes..

Right now, I’m focused on mastering HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I enjoy "vibe coding" at home—taking what we learn at school, like PHP and XAMPP, and practicing it on my own. I like building simple things, like login pages, just to see if I can turn what I learn in class into something real and working.

I’ve discovered that I really like the technical side of things. There’s something satisfying about figuring out how a few lines of code can create a professional-looking website. I’m a big fan of minimalism—keeping things simple, clean, and easy to use.

My time at J.H. Cerilles State College really opened my eyes. It gave me the foundation I needed to realize that IT is actually fun. Now, I spend a lot of my free time experimenting with new layouts and trying to keep up with the latest trends in web design.

Looking ahead, my goal is to become a Full-Stack Developer. I’m excited about where the web is going, and I’m dedicated to perfecting the front-end, one div at a time, until I’m ready to build entire systems from scratch.
